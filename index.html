About Lakhani Motors

Founded in 2000, Lakhani Motors has grown from its humble beginnings into a prestigious international dealership with a head office in Japan and thriving showrooms in Uganda, Kenya, and Dubai. Established by Asif Memon Lakhani and Abubakar Memon Lakhani, our brand was built on the foundation of trust, reliability, and excellence in service.

With over 2,000 vehicles sold, we proudly cater to a wide spectrum of clients — from first-time buyers to luxury enthusiasts — always delivering quality and peace of mind. Our services extend beyond sales, offering competitive financing options, after-sales support, and personalized consultation to make every purchase seamless.

At Lakhani Motors, we believe prestige is not just in the cars we sell, but in the trusted experience we provide. Our team ensures every client enjoys unmatched value, best prices, and a relationship built on transparency. Whether in Uganda, Kenya, or Dubai, we are committed to setting the gold standard in automotive excellence.

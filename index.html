About Lakhani Motors

Lakhani Motors (U) Limited, registered in Uganda on 12th August 2005, is a premier luxury automobile dealership with operations spanning Japan, Dubai, Uganda, and Kenya. Founded by Abubakar Memon (99% shareholder) and Madiha Abubakar (1% shareholder), the company has grown over 20 years to redefine automotive luxury.

Our mission is to provide high-quality vehicles and unmatched customer service, generating revenue, employment, and contributing to local economies. Our bonded warehouse W0326 spans 1.35 acres on Jinja Road.

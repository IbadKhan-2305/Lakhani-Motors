About Lakhani Motors

Excellence in Luxury Automobiles Since 1985

Our Legacy

Lakhani Motors is a premier automobile dealership group with over 40 years of excellence in the automotive industry. Established with a vision to redefine luxury car ownership, we take pride in delivering world-class vehicles and unmatched customer service.

Global Presence

  • Japan (Head Office) — Serving since 1985 with 40+ years of business excellence.
  • Dubai, UAE — Established in 2000 to cater to the Middle East luxury market.
  • Uganda — Expanded in 2005 to serve East African customers.
  • Kenya — Opened in 2015 as part of our African growth strategy.

Our Mission

To provide our clients with exclusive, high-quality vehicles that combine performance, luxury, and reliability — while ensuring a seamless customer journey.

Our Vision

To remain a trusted name in the automotive world, constantly evolving to meet the demands of luxury and performance seekers worldwide.
